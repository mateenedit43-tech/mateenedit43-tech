About Toyo Toys

We believe every child deserves a childhood filled with wonder, creativity, and joy.

Where Imagination Takes Flight

Toyo Toys was born from a simple idea: that the best toys don't just entertain — they inspire. Founded in 2018 by a group of parents and educators who wanted more for their children, we set out to curate a collection of toys that spark curiosity, build skills, and create lasting memories.

We travel the world to find the most innovative, sustainable, and beautifully designed toys. From classic wooden puzzles to cutting-edge STEM kits, every product in our store is chosen with care — because we know that the toys we give our children shape the way they see the world.

Today, Toyo Toys is proud to be a trusted name for families everywhere. We're not just a toy store — we're a community of dreamers, builders, and believers in the power of play.
